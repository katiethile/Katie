    • You're probably thinking, "What are perlers?" Well, perlers are basically colored beads put together to be a design that you want, placing them together one by one, on a board, then heating them together so that they stick together with the help of parchment paper, double sided tape and an iron. You go over the beads with the parchment paper on top with the iron on medium heat until you see that the beads stick to the paper, then flip it over and do the same to the other side.
